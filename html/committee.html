About the Committee

The organising committee of AGA 2019 is the ASEAN Youth Organization of Australia (AYO Australia).

ASEAN Games Australia (AGA) is an annual sporting festival that aims to gather all lovers of sport. Through AGA, we hope to create new friendships and promote camaraderie between individuals from different cultural backgrounds, thereby creating greater cultural understanding between the ASEAN communities. Individuals of different athletic skill with the same passion and interest in sports can gather together to have fun and enjoy a friendly game or two. AGA is a perfect way to showcase your sportsmanship and teamwork alongside your ASEAN family, a home away from home. AGA also aims to nurture future leaders of ASEAN, one who strives for holistic achievement and is able to develop interpersonal skills and teamwork. With our extensive sponsors and partners, lucky draws with attractive prizes can be won too. Not to mention, cash prizes to be won for top 3 players of each sport!
